Russia massive attack Kiev

Date: July 2, 2026

Russian forces attacked with drones last night at around 11 pm Moscow time, and according to media reports, buildings and companies manufacturing drones and "Flamingo" cruise missiles were targeted. Russian forces also disrupted air defenses before the missiles were fired, which later remained paralyzed because all missiles were directed at the drones. "Zircon", "Kalibr" and Iskander missiles were used. According to Ukrainian media, 10 people were killed and several were wounded. Many buildings were damaged due to the effects of outdated air defense missiles. The reason for this Russian action is that Ukraine is trying to target everyone with a lot of drones and cruise missiles. Before this attack, there were attacks on gas stations where they discovered that tanker trucks were being driven and filled there.
