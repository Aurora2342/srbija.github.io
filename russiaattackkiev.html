Russian forces attacked with drones last night at around 11 pm Moscow time, and according to media reports, buildings and companies manufacturing drones and "Flamingo" cruise missiles were targeted. Russian forces also disrupted air defenses before the missiles were fired, which later remained paralyzed because all missiles were directed at the drones. "Zircon", "Kalibr" and Iskander missiles were used. According to Ukrainian media, 10 people were killed and several were wounded, and many buildings were damaged due to the effects of outdated air defense missiles.
