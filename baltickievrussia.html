Baltic help kiev to attack Russia

Date: July 4, 2026

Last night, the Baltic states helped Kyiv strike Russia with drones—but why? They view it as an act of revenge against Moscow, given that their national identities were built on hatred toward Russia and Russians themselves; earlier this year, we witnessed the persecution of the Russian Church in Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania. Lithuania has never gotten over the loss of Kaliningrad after World War II and seeks revenge against Russia at every turn, while Estonia and Latvia feel "threatened" due to paranoia. The stance adopted by these three states played right into the hands of the West, ultimately leading to their entry into NATO in the early 21st century.
